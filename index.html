    Choco de Luxe – Sales Performance Analysis Objective using Tableau

    For this project, I used Tableau to analyze Choco de Luxe’s sales performance across locations, channels, products, and salespeople. My goal was to uncover actionable insights that support profitable growth and operational efficiency. Key findings include: Top Locations & Channels: High-performing locations like Piedmont and leading channels such as the Website show that strong execution paired with market reach drives results. Opportunities for Improvement: Some locations and channels generate revenue but lag in delivery efficiency or profitability, highlighting areas for better logistics and cost control. Workforce Insights: Profit targets can be met even with lower sales volumes when salespeople focus on high-margin products and pricing discipline, emphasizing the need to align incentives and training with profitability objectives. Product Performance: Non-organic products drive most revenue, while organic products, despite lower volumes, deliver higher margins and support premium positioning. The Tableau dashboards I developed provide business leaders with an intuitive, visual way to track performance, optimize strategy, and make data-driven decisions.

    EMPLOYEE PERFORMANCE AND SUCCESS INSIGHT using SQL

    For this project, I used SQL to extract, clean, and analyze employee performance, retention, and compensation data to generate actionable HR insights. My analysis revealed that Marketing and Engineering had the highest turnover, suggesting workload, engagement, or growth challenges. I also identified 71 employees scoring below 3.5, highlighting areas for targeted coaching, while 9 high performers scored a perfect 5.0, indicating key talent worth retaining. The compensation review showed a total salary expense of ₦4.85M, with performance generally linked to pay, though inconsistencies exist across departments. Key Recommendations: Strengthen retention with targeted surveys and engagement programs Boost performance through mentorship, training, and goal-setting initiatives Reward excellence with bonuses, promotions, or recognition programs Ensure fair compensation via regular salary reviews Adopt data-driven HR strategies to track retention, performance, and pay equity This project demonstrates my ability to use SQL to turn raw HR data into actionable insights, helping organizations improve employee engagement, retention, and overall workforce performance..
